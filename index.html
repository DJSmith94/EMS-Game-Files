RF Tug of War

Choose your RF battle

Configure your data carrier and data receive modem during prep, then use the spectrum analyzer to find the opposing transponder and build interference signals. First file to complete wins.

PrepFive minutes to pick your data modem and set your own 36 MHz transponder plan.
BattleThe analyzer can expand to the full 80 MHz view. The chosen data modem stays selected, but the data RX must be retuned after carrier or format changes.
ScoringAn 8 Gb pseudo file advances only while the data RX is locked. Eb/No, FEC loss, and receiver tuning control progress.
